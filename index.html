The Restaurant

Inspired by Andy Warhol and the New York City lifestyle, Silver Factory is a place for creative gathering where local artists and creators socialize over delicious Korean fusion tapas. Our chefs explore fusian cuisines that incorporate the flavors of the vibrant and international city.

Located right under the Williambsurg Bridge, this hidden restaurant boasts fresh flavor, impressive cocktails, and a versatile environment that seamlessly transitions from trendy cocktail bar to evening event space. Silver Factory has a private event space in the back that can be used for private bookings or larger parties. The space has hosted everything from podcast tapings and comedy shows to monthly DJ dance parties.
